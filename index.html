Muhammad ibn Musa al-Khwarizmi

The reason why the western world has a decimal positional number system

Al-Khwārizmī's The Compendious Book on Calculation by Completion and Balancing presented the first systematic solution of linear and quadratic equations in Arabic. Because he is the first to teach algebra as an independent discipline and introduced the methods of "reduction" and "balancing". He has been described as the father or founder of algebra.
